About us

Our agency specializes in organizing small-group tours to Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, and other destinations in the region. Our itineraries are carefully crafted to showcase the best of each destination, from the ancient Silk Road cities of Samarkand and Bukhara to the stunning alpine landscapes of the Tien Shan mountains. With our experienced local guides, comfortable accommodations, and personalized service, you can sit back, relax, and enjoy your journey through Central Asia.

But we're more than just a tour operator. We also offer expert travel advice and assistance for independent travelers who want to explore Central Asia on their own. Our team of travel experts can help you plan your itinerary, book your accommodations and transportation, and provide you with all the information and resources you need to make the most of your trip. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, we're here to help you make the most of your Central Asia experience.
